Items of the Trade

The tutorial found in Phasmophobia is great for learning the very basic items but once you've completed a few missions you are gonna find that there are plenty more things. The journal in the game gives brief desciptions for each item but here you'll get a more in depth look at how each item fucntions and if it's worth using. The itmes have been split into categories to help break them up into their main functions.

  • Light Sources
  • Basic Detection
  • Sound Detection
  • Motion Detection
  • Cameras
  • Communication
  • Attack Prevention
